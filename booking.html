How to Book

Please complete the form below with as much detail as possible. I typically respond to booking requests within 24 hours. For last-minute requests, please allow at least 2 hours for a response.

Screening Process

To ensure mutual safety and comfort, all new clients undergo a discreet screening process. This may include verification of identity and references from other established providers. Your information is always handled with the utmost discretion.

Deposit & Confirmation

A 20% deposit is required to secure all bookings, which is applied toward your donation. Once your deposit is received, you'll receive a confirmation with the details of our appointment.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations with more than 48 hours notice will receive a full deposit refund. Less than 48 hours notice will result in forfeiture of the deposit. I understand that emergencies happen, and I'm always willing to work with respectful clients.
